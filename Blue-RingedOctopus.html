Blue-Ringed Octopus


Description:

8 short arms, blue-rings or mottled pattern for camouflage. Direct threat to humans. Saliva contains the potentially lethal neurotoxin tetrodotoxin.

Treatment:

  • Pressure bandage to the bite site
  • Artificial respiration once the paralysis has disabled the victim's respiratory muscles
  • Call the Poisons Information Centre on 13 11 26
