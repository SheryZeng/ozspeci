Common Brown Snake


Description:

Usually brown body. Dangerously venomous and bite can be fatal. Body colour varies, usually brown. Young snakes have a black head and occasionally dark stripes across the body. Up to 2 m long.

Treatment:

  • Pressure bandage to the bite site
  • Victim move as little as possible
  • Call an ambulance (000) to take the person to the nearest hospital
