Redback Spider


Description:

Black spider with a red stripe on top of the abdomen and a red hour-glass marking underneath. Bite can be highly venomous. Symptoms include localised increasing pain and sweating at the bite site, muscular weakness, loss of coordination, nausea, vomiting and convulsions.

Treatment:

  • Wash the area and keep it clean
  • If symptoms are severe, go to the nearest hospital
  • Do NOT apply a pressure immobilisation bandage
