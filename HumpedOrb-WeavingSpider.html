Humped Orb-Weaving Spider


Description:

Cephalothorax black and covered with silvery hair with bumps on the sternum, long black-brown legs with yellowed banded joints, abdomen large and grey. Female: cephalothorax is black and partially covered with silvery hairs and a pair of small bumps in the middle of the dorsal surface. Males are much smaller and more brownish overall.

Treatment:

  • Wash the area and keep it clean
  • If symptoms are severe, go to the nearest hospital
  • Do NOT apply a pressure immobilisation bandage
